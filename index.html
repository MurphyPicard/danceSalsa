'Salsa Classes NYC' draws on Mambo, Afro-Cuban, Latin folklore, jazz, and modern dance when crafting its choreography. With so many influences, the studio's focus might seem muddled. Professional dancer and studio owner Manny Davis, however, has two goals: for the dances to complement the music they're performed too and for every move to come from the dancers' hearts.

Mission: Created, inspirational, heal through movement as well as the sustainability of the art. Allow people to come and be free in a safe and creative space. Help NYC residents find the best dancer within their-self, while connecting to others in a unique venue.

Even though it is a beginner class a lot of focus will be made on mastering the salsa dance skills also to stretching in the beginning of the class, exercises how to keep balance when moving and turning, and one of the most important things is connection to the music and your partner.



The classes will take place Thursdays from 8pm - 9:30pm and Sundays from 4pm - 5:30pm (subject to change) in Midtown Manhattan. It is a 1 hour 30 min class with a break in the middle.

BEGINNER CLASS:

  • Learn the basics to get you on the dance floor
  • Learn to do the basic step (On 2)
  • Learn turns and crossovers with a partner
  • Learn shines and solo patterns
  • Learn foot and hand movements for women and men
  • And learn to do it with a smile!
  • No partners needed
  • A relaxing way to meet new friends and get your body moving!
